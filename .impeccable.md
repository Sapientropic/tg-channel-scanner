# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users

T-Sense is used by one or a few technical power users who monitor many Telegram channels and need a fast local review surface for job leads, market/news signals, or other high-noise channel workflows. The dashboard is a daily operational console, not a marketing surface.

### Brand Personality

Premium retro, local-first, signal-focused. The UI should feel like a careful workstation for deciding what deserves action today.

### Aesthetic Direction

Use the existing premium retro pixel intelligence console direction: dark local-machine shell, warm report-paper panels, crisp 1px borders, hard pixel shadows, amber/teal/rust/blue semantic accents, and dense scan-friendly visual hierarchy. Keep the dashboard distinct from generic SaaS cards.

### Design Principles

- Actions before explanation: primary surfaces should show what to review, export, run, or fix next.
- Visualization before prose: use status bars, compact counters, and risk badges instead of long explanatory copy.
- ADHD-friendly focus: do not repeat the same count, lane id, report title, or diagnostic sentence across adjacent surfaces; default to one judgment, one visual shape, and one next action.
- Local privacy is visible but quiet: show that tokens and raw text stay local without exposing secrets or raw Telegram content.
- Review cards and source decisions are the product surface; settings should not become a dumping ground for diagnostics.
- Desktop layouts must open cleanly at 1360/1440 widths without path-looking labels, long source names, or 0-value metrics dominating the screen.
